Our Story

His Version

I just couldn't work up the courage to talk to the cute girl in my Camp War Eagle (CWE) group, and when I did, it wasn't really my smoothest line. For many people with missed connections in CWE, this would be the end of the story. It certainly would have been, if not for a mutual friend and a bit of luck.

For a while, we were only great friends, but a trip to Nashville with friends left us looking at each other in a different light.

Dating in college was easy, we had (and still have) great friends and many fond memories — but after moving to NYC and and doing some old fashioned long distance dating (made so difficult because I hate the phone) — I was ready for her to move to NYC.

With a bit of fortune on our side a job presented itself, after a year and a half of living in the same city, I knew the time was right to take it to the next level.
